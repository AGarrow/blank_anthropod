How permissions work

The permissions system is centered around organizations for the time being, such that you can edit a person's details if you have permission to edit any organization the person is a member of. Having permission to edit an organization also confers the ability to create new people as members of the organization. The remaining actions are only available to admins:

  • creating and deleting organizations
  • deleting people
  • adding an existing person as a member of an existing org
  • creating a new standalone person (i.e., not as a member of an org)
  • deleting memberships
